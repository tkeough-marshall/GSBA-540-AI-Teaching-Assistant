About This Project

GSBA 540 AI Teaching Assistant
Part of the USC Marshall AI Strategy Initiative

Developed to support GSBA 540: Contemporary Issues in Competitive Strategy (Spring 2025), taught by Professor Florenta Teodoridis with support from Ty Keough, Teaching Assistant.

This assistant blends retrieval-augmented artificial intelligence models with verified course materials to create an intelligent, contextual learning companion. It enhances strategic thinking by helping students connect concepts, frameworks, and cases in real time.

Principles
• Operates within the academic and ethical framework of the course.
• Maintains an executive-level standard of clarity, professionalism, and analytical rigor.
• Uses only verified course content and does not fabricate information.
• Defers grading, policy, and administrative matters to Faculty.

This project advances USC Marshall’s commitment to responsible AI integration in business education, showcasing augmentation, transparency, and human-centered design in practice.
