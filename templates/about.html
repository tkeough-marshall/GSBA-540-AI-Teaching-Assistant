About This Project

MOR 565 AI Teaching Assistant
Part of the USC Marshall AI Strategy Initiative

Developed to support MOR 565: Alliances & Cooperative Strategy (Spring 2025), taught by Professor Kyle Mayer with support from Ty Keough, Teaching Assistant, at the USC Marshall School of Business.

This assistant blends retrieval-augmented language models with verified course materials to create an intelligent, contextual learning companion. It enhances strategic thinking and reflection — not assignment completion — by helping students connect concepts, frameworks, and cases in real time.

Principles
• Operates within the academic and ethical framework of the course.
• Maintains an MBA-level standard of clarity, professionalism, and analytical rigor.
• Uses only verified course content and does not fabricate information.
• Defers grading, policy, and administrative matters to Professor Mayer.

This project advances USC Marshall’s commitment to responsible AI integration in graduate business education — showcasing augmentation, transparency, and human-centered design in practice.

Created and monitored by Ty Keough, USC Marshall MS Finance Class of 2026.
